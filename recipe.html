Nigerian Indomie Noodles

This authentic Nigerian Indomie recipe showcases one of Nigeria's most beloved comfort foods. With its rich, savory flavors and hearty additions, Indomie has become a staple in Nigerian households and street food culture. Follow this guide to create the perfect Nigerian Indomie that's both satisfying and delicious!

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 3 minutes

Cooking time: 5 minutes

Number of servings: 1 person

Difficulty level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Indomie instant noodles (any flavor)
  • 400ml water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Optional: corned beef or sardines
  • Optional: scotch bonnet pepper (for heat)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add sliced onions and minced garlic, saut√© until fragrant and golden (about 2 minutes).
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and bell peppers, cook until tomatoes break down (3-4 minutes).
  4. Meanwhile, boil water in a separate pot and cook Indomie noodles for 2 minutes (slightly underdone).
  5. Drain noodles, reserving 1/4 cup of cooking water, then add noodles to the vegetable pan.
  6. Add the Indomie seasoning packets and reserved cooking water, toss everything together.
  7. Create space in the pan and scramble the eggs, then mix them into the noodles.
  8. Add corned beef or sardines if using, cook for another 1-2 minutes until heated through.
  9. Season with salt, pepper, and garnish with green onions before serving hot.

Tips & Notes

For the most authentic Nigerian style, don't be afraid to let the noodles get slightly crispy in the pan - this adds great texture! The key is balancing the vegetables with the noodles. Many Nigerians add corned beef or canned sardines for extra protein and flavor. Adjust the scotch bonnet pepper to your heat preference, and always serve immediately while hot for the best experience!
